About Us

Bros.Bespoke, first founded in the basement of Guanghua building by a group of Peking University Class'14 students, has now grown rapidly to an O2O enterprise focusing on bespoke business dressings, with a professional service team, an online shopping platform, and an off-line experiencing spot.

We have noticed that, trying out one shirt after another in department stores is an outdated, inefficient and tiring way to find a satisfactory piece of garment. Yet despite shopping online offers a much more convenient shopping experience, a perfect fit is never to be guaranteed.

That is what inspires us to start up, to save your labor by ordering online while providing suits of tailored quality. In the past year, we have been striving for perfection. Our perspiration is appreciated by the body of our alumni, and dozens of Peking University professors. In the future, we are dedicated to delicate and considerate services continuously. We sincerely hope that our bespoke suits accompany the friends who honor us with the opportunity, in every great moment of their lives.

Your bespoke life begins here.
